+ Minecraft Sculpture: + Plague culture

This piece was an exploration of the inter-historical interaction of our culture's current re-visitation of plagues of the past to better understand and predict our functions within a global pandemic. It approaches the plague mask as a cultural object belonging to two distinct cultures at once.
